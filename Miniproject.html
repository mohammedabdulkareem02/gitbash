FAQ'S

1. How do I search for flights?

Our flight search is simple. Visit our homepage and enter your departure and destination cities, travel dates, and the number of passengers. Click "Search," and we'll provide you with a list of available flights that match your criteria.

2. How do I book a flight?

Once you've found a flight you're interested in, click "Select" or "Book." Follow the prompts to enter passenger information and payment details. After completing the booking, you'll receive a confirmation email with your itinerary.

3. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit and debit cards, as well as some online payment platforms. The available options will be shown during the booking process.
